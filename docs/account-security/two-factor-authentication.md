# Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a second form of verification in addition to your password.

## Enable 2FA

1. Log in to your CoinByte account
2. Go to Security Settings
3. Click on "Enable 2FA"
4. Follow the setup wizard

## Recommended 2FA Methods

We support multiple 2FA methods:

- Google Authenticator (Recommended)
- SMS Authentication
- Email Authentication

## Backup Codes

Always save your backup codes in a secure location. You'll need them if you lose access to your 2FA device.
