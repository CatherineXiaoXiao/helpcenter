# Modify Account Information

Learn how to safely update your profile, contact information, and account preferences on CoinByte.

## Accessing Your Account Settings

To access your account settings:

1. Log in to your CoinByte account
2. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner
3. Select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu
4. Authenticate with your password or 2FA if prompted

## Updating Personal Information

### Profile Details

You can update the following profile information:

1. Navigate to "Account Settings" > "Profile"
2. Update your information:
   - Full name
   - Date of birth
   - Occupation
   - Country of residence
3. Click "Save Changes"
4. Verify your identity using 2FA or email confirmation

Note: Some information may require additional verification if changed.

### Profile Picture

To update your profile picture:

1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Profile"
2. Click on your current profile image
3. Upload a new image (JPG, PNG formats, max 5MB)
4. Crop and adjust as needed
5. Click "Save"

## Managing Contact Information

### Email Address

To update your email address:

1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Security"
2. Click "Change" next to your email address
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify with your current email and 2FA
5. Check your new email for a verification link
6. Click the verification link to complete the change

### Phone Number

To update your phone number:

1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Security"
2. Click "Change" next to your phone number
3. Enter your new phone number with country code
4. Verify with 2FA or current phone SMS verification
5. Enter the verification code sent to your new number

## Communication Preferences

### Notification Settings

Customize your notification preferences:

1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Notifications"
2. Toggle on/off different notification types:
   - Security alerts
   - Transaction confirmations
   - Price alerts
   - News and updates
   - Marketing communications
3. Choose delivery methods (email, SMS, push notifications)
4. Save your preferences

### Language and Regional Settings

To change your language or regional settings:

1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Preferences"
2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown
3. Choose your time zone
4. Select your preferred currency display format
5. Click "Save"

## Privacy Settings

Manage your privacy settings:

1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Privacy"
2. Configure your privacy options:
   - Trading history visibility
   - Profile visibility to other users
   - Third-party data sharing preferences
3. Review and update your consent settings
4. Save changes

## Linking External Accounts

### Link or Unlink Social Accounts

1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Connected Accounts"
2. View currently linked accounts
3. Click "Link" next to the platform you want to connect
4. Follow the authentication process
5. To unlink, click "Unlink" and confirm the action

## Important Security Considerations

When modifying account information:

- Changes to critical information trigger security notifications
- Major changes may require enhanced verification
- There may be a 24-48 hour security hold on withdrawals after significant changes
- Keep all contact information current for account recovery purposes

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I can't change certain information. Why?

Some information, particularly KYC details, may be locked after verification to prevent fraud. Contact support if you need to update critical identity information.

### How long do changes take to process?

Most changes take effect immediately after verification. However:
- Email changes require verification of both old and new addresses
- Phone number changes require verification of the new number
- Major changes may require manual review (1-3 business days)

### Will changing my information affect my account verification status?

Minor updates typically won't affect your verification status. However, significant changes to identity information may require re-verification of your account.
