Create an account

This article will guide you through creating your account. You can create an account using either our mobile app or the web platform.

  • Mobile App
  • Web Platform

Create an account via the mobile app​

  1. Download the app: Download our mobile application from the relevant app store.

  2. Open the app and tap 'Sign Up'.
  3. Enter your details: Fill in your email address, create a strong password, enter your phone number, and select your country of residence.
  4. Agree to Terms: Read and agree to the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
  5. Verify Email: Check your email inbox for a verification link and click on it. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.
  6. Verify Phone: You'll receive an SMS with a code. Enter this code in the app to verify your phone number.
  7. Secure Your Account: Set up your preferred method for securing your account (e.g., PIN, Biometrics).
  8. Account Created: Your account is now set up! You can proceed with identity verification.

Create an account via the web platform​

  1. Go to the Registration Page: Navigate to our website's registration page.
  2. Enter Your Details: Provide your email address, create a secure password, enter your phone number, and select your country.
  3. Agree to Terms: Review and accept the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
  4. Click 'Create My Account'.
  5. Verify Email: Look for a verification email and click the link inside. Check spam if needed.
  6. Verify Phone: Enter the verification code sent to your phone via SMS.
  7. Account Created: Your account is ready. The next step is identity verification.

If you encounter any issues during the account creation process, please contact our Support Team.
