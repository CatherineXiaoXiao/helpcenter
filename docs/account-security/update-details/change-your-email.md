## When should I update my email?

<TickList>
  <li>Your email is no longer active</li>
  <li>You want to improve your security</li>
</TickList>

---

## How to change your email

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Log in to your CoinByte account</li>
  <li>Go to Account Settings > Email</li>
  <li>Enter your new email and confirm</li>
  <li>Click the verification link sent to your inbox</li>
</RoundStepList>
