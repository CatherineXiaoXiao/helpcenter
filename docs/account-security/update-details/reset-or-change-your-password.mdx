Regularly updating your password enhances security and prevents unauthorized access.  
Weak or outdated passwords can make your account more susceptible to compromise.

Learn more about <a href="/helpcenter/docs/account-security/create-coinbyte-account/password-requirements-and-management"><strong>Password Requirements and Management</strong></a> to create the strongest possible password.

---

## Reset a Forgotten Password

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Forgotten your password?</strong> from the sign-in page.</li>
  <li>Type your registered email address.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>get a code</strong>, you will receive an email to <strong>get the verification code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Paste your <strong>email verification code</strong> and <strong>Google verification code</strong> into the CoinByte app. Click <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter and save a new password before returning to the sign-in page.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Those who forget the password</strong> from the sign-in page.</li>
  <li>Type your registered email address.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Get code</strong>, you will receive an email to get the <strong>verification code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Paste your <strong>email verification code</strong> and <strong>Google verification code</strong> into the CoinByte website. Click <strong>Confirmation</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter and save a new password before returning to the sign-in page.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
