# Complete your KYC Verification

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required to ensure the security of our platform and comply with regulations.

## Individual Account Verification

### Required Documents
1. Valid government-issued ID (Passport, Driver's License)
2. Proof of address (Utility bill, Bank statement)
3. Recent photo of yourself

### Steps to Complete KYC
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select "Verification"
3. Choose "Individual Account"
4. Follow the on-screen instructions
5. Upload required documents
6. Submit for review

## Corporate Account Verification

### Required Documents
1. Business registration documents
2. Company address proof
3. Director's ID
4. Beneficial owner information
5. Corporate structure documentation

### Steps to Complete
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select "Verification"
3. Choose "Corporate Account"
4. Upload required documents
5. Provide additional information as requested
6. Submit for review

## Verification Status

- Pending: Documents under review
- Approved: Full access granted
- Rejected: Additional information needed

## Processing Time
- Individual accounts: 1-2 business days
- Corporate accounts: 3-5 business days
