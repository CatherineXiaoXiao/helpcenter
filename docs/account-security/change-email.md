You can change the email address linked to your CoinByte account in just a few steps.

> 💡 **Important**: This process requires access to both your current and new email inboxes.

---

## 🔄 Steps to Change Your Email

1. **Log in** to your CoinByte account.
2. Click on your profile icon and go to **Account Settings**.
3. Select the **Email** section.
4. Click **Edit** next to your current email address.
5. Enter your **new email address** and confirm.
6. A **verification email** will be sent to your new email.
7. Click the verification link in that email to complete the update.

---

## 📧 Didn’t receive the email?

- Check your **Spam** or **Promotions** folder.
- Make sure you typed your new email address correctly.
- You can resend the confirmation from the same settings page.

---

## 🛠 Need Help?

If you're unable to access your old email address or experience issues during the process, please [contact our support team](https://cs.coinbyte.com) for manual assistance.
