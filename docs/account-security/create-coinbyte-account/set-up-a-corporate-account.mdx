CoinByte allows businesses and entities to create a **corporate account** for cryptocurrency trading under a company name.

If you're looking to create a **personal account**, see [Register a CoinByte Account](/helpcenter/docs/account-security/create-coinbyte-account/register-a-coinbyte-account).

---

## Eligibility

<InfoBox>
You must be a **registered business** with documentation to support your corporate identity, directors, and beneficial ownership.
</InfoBox>

---

## How to apply for a corporate account

<RoundStepList>
  <li><strong>Register a personal CoinByte account</strong> first using your email address and identity. This account will serve as the base.</li>
  <li><strong>Submit a request</strong> to our [Support Team](/contact-support) with the subject "Corporate Account Application".</li>
  <li>Prepare and submit the following documentation:
    <ul>
      <li>Certificate of Incorporation</li>
      <li>Company Constitution or Articles of Association</li>
      <li>Director and Shareholder ID (passport or driver’s license)</li>
      <li>Proof of business address (utility bill or bank statement)</li>
      <li>Company extract (issued within the last 3 months)</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Wait for review</strong> and confirmation from our compliance team. They may request further information.</li>
</RoundStepList>

---

## Notes

<WarningBox>
Make sure all documents are clear, valid, and in English. Non-English documents must be accompanied by certified translations.
</WarningBox>

---

## Once approved

<TickList>
  <li>You will receive a confirmation email and your corporate account status will be activated.</li>
  <li>Trading limits and features may vary based on the business type and jurisdiction.</li>
  <li>For ongoing support, you may request account management services via [Pro Services](/support/pro-services).</li>
</TickList>
