Create your CoinByte account today to access a broad selection of cryptocurrencies with competitively low trading fees, all on a secure and user-friendly platform.

Once your account is set up, explore the world of crypto with CoinByte Learn, or discover more about CoinByte in the Get Started section.

If you wish to create an entity account, please refer to [Set up a company account](/account-security/set-up-company-account).

<InfoBox>
⚠️ **Note**: You can only create a CoinByte account from within Australia or New Zealand.
</InfoBox>

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">

## Register via the Mobile App

<RoundStepList>
 <li>Download the CoinByte app.</li>
  <li>Open the app and select 'Sign Up'.</li>
  <li>
    Enter your email address and create a password.
    For password tips, check out our <a href="/account-security/password-requirements">Password requirements and management</a>.
  </li>
  <li>Verify your email address by inputting the verification code sent to your email address.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="web" label="Web Platform">

## Register via the Web Platform

<RoundStepList>
 <li>Click 'Get Started' or 'Sign Up' on the homepage.</li>
  <li>Enter your email address and create a password. For password tips, check out our [Password requirements and management](/account-security/password-requirements).</li>
  <li>Verify your email address by inputting the verification code sent to your email address.</li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

<TickList>
  <li>Set up two-factor authentication.</li>
  <li>Complete your KYC Verification (individual account).</li>
  <li>Start trading on CoinByte.</li>
</TickList>
