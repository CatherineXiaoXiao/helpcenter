# Password Recovery and Reset

Learn how to safely recover your account access when you've forgotten your password.

## Forgot Password Process

If you've forgotten your CoinByte account password, follow these steps to reset it:

1. Go to the CoinByte login page
2. Click on the "Forgot Password?" link
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Solve the security verification (CAPTCHA)
5. Click "Submit" to request a password reset link

## Password Reset Methods

CoinByte offers multiple methods to reset your password, depending on your account security settings:

### Email Reset Method

1. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam/junk folders if necessary)
2. Click on the reset link (valid for 30 minutes only)
3. Create a new password following the security requirements
4. Confirm your new password
5. Complete the reset process by logging in with your new password

### Two-Factor Authentication Reset

If you have 2FA enabled:

1. Follow the email reset steps above
2. After clicking the reset link, you'll need to verify your identity with your 2FA method
3. Enter the verification code from your authenticator app or SMS
4. Create and confirm your new password
5. Log in with your new credentials

## Account Recovery for Lost 2FA

If you've lost access to your two-factor authentication device:

1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email and follow the initial steps
3. On the 2FA verification page, click "Lost access to 2FA device?"
4. Choose one of the recovery options:
   - Use backup recovery codes (if previously saved)
   - Complete additional identity verification
5. Follow the guided recovery process
6. Reset both your password and 2FA method

## Security Considerations

For your protection, the following security measures apply to password resets:

- Password reset links expire after 30 minutes
- Multiple failed reset attempts may trigger additional verification
- Account login will be temporarily locked after multiple incorrect password attempts
- You'll receive email notifications about password changes
- All devices will be signed out after a password reset

## Additional Verification

In some cases, especially for accounts with high value or suspicious activity patterns, additional verification may be required:

1. Identity document verification
2. Selfie verification
3. Waiting period before full account access is restored
4. Review by our security team

## Preventive Measures

To avoid password recovery issues:

- Keep your email address and phone number up to date
- Save your 2FA backup codes in a secure location
- Consider using a password manager
- Enable multiple recovery methods
- Regularly review your account security settings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I didn't receive the password reset email. What should I do?

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Add support@coinbyte.com to your contacts
- Try the process again after 5 minutes
- Contact customer support if issues persist

### How long does account recovery take?

- Standard password reset: Immediate to 24 hours
- 2FA recovery: 1-3 business days
- Enhanced verification cases: 3-7 business days

### Is there a limit to password reset attempts?

Yes, for security reasons, we limit the number of reset attempts within a 24-hour period. If you exceed this limit, your account recovery will require manual review by our security team.
