Download or update the CoinByte app for the **latest features**, **security enhancements**, and **smooth user experience**. This article covers how to get the app on both iOS and Android devices.

<InfoBox>
For desktop access, you can also use the [CoinByte Web Platform](/getting-started/introduction).
</InfoBox>

---

## How to download or update

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="ios" label="Apple App Store">
<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

### iOS Devices

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<RoundStepList>
  <li>
    <strong>Open the App Store</strong> on your iPhone or iPad.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Search for “CoinByte”</strong> in the search bar.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Tap “Get” or “Update”.</strong> If you've already installed the app, tapping “Update” will upgrade it to the latest version.
  </li>
  <li>
    You can also click the link below to go directly to the app:
    <br/>
    <a href="https://apps.apple.com/app/idxxxxxxxxx" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">
      <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1418700788/967cd195f03711ba8f8b1f17cb27/badge-example-preferred.png" alt="Download on the App Store" width="160" />
    </a>
  </li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="android" label="Google Play">
<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

### Android Devices

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
<RoundStepList>
  <li>
    <strong>Open the Google Play Store</strong> on your Android phone or tablet.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Search for “CoinByte”</strong> in the search bar.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Tap “Install” or “Update”.</strong> If the app is already installed, tapping “Update” ensures it’s the latest version.
  </li>
  <li>
    You can also click the link below to go directly to the app:
    <br/>
    <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=xxxxxxxxx" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">
      <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1418701198/04bca9d95c219d420b8a3c4f785e/google.png" alt="Get it on Google Play" width="160" />
    </a>
  </li>
</RoundStepList>

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Tips for success

<TickList>
  <li>Ensure your device’s operating system is up to date.</li>
  <li>Connect to a stable internet connection when downloading or updating.</li>
  <li>If you encounter issues, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app.</li>
</TickList>

<WarningBox>
If you still experience issues downloading or updating the app, please contact our [Support Team](/contact-support).
</WarningBox>
