Your phone number is also one of the methods of **2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)**.
<InfoBox>
After you verify your Phone Number, you will need to <a href="/helpcenter/docs/account-security/verification-and-security/complete-your-KYC-verification-(individual-account)"><strong>Complete your KYC Verification (individual account)</strong></a> or <a href="/helpcenter/docs/account-security/verification-and-security/complete-your-KYC-verification-(corporate-account)"><strong>Complete your KYC Verification (corporate account)</strong></a>.
</InfoBox>

---

## How to verify your phone number

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">
  <div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select the <strong>green CoinByte icon</strong> <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1555779819/52d74b8696fefcd6dceb554af349/logo_app.png?expires=1749168000&signature=20232987de17ef05104405425e5463116cf3cee656ffa364855b833a96064a56&req=dSUiE855lIleUPMW3Hu4gb1ouEWCZB41SM5%2F2HeE9AaeUUQttbK1AUgJYzo4%0AYA%3D%3D%0A" alt="CoinByte App Icon" width="20" /> in the top left to open the Personal Profile page.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Security Settings</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Cellphone verification</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter your phone number and click <strong>Get a code</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the one-time passcode sent to the phone number and select <strong>Next Step</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

 
  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="web" label="Web">
  <div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1555803163/42c65a29f4a29401299291309f7a/web_center.png?expires=1749168000&signature=b4bc2817fdaa707604a0ce759b4136bd1351bce22cf74e95381bbe318db46a5f&req=dSUiE8F%2BnoBZWvMW3Hu4ga9r38GE4gnmKNrKHCYOqoMc7BMagqtGe9c7pChl%0Apg%3D%3D%0A" alt="Web Profile Icon" width="24" /> <strong>Personal Profile Center</strong> in the header.</li>

      <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1556252436/ffd366968904b33c8657a58dbec9/818fcfefde1aa1b0b0cde4efa0b891a.png?expires=1749069000&signature=99550ef2e055ffef2cca8d3bc7db041d722237e98e807df4a577a54c32ab9941&req=dSUiEMt7n4VcX%2FMW1HO4zXQZyjAjF4WrUte8RjiGZYuR0tqulDVNXmF9iUCU%0AWZhG%0A" alt="Step 4 Screenshot" width="400" />

    <figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
