# Verification Guide

Learn about CoinByte's verification process and requirements to ensure your account is compliant and secure.

## Verification Levels

CoinByte offers a tiered verification system to meet various trading needs and regulatory requirements:

### Level 1 - Basic Verification
- Email verification
- Phone number verification
- Trading limits: Up to 2 BTC per day

### Level 2 - Identity Verification
- Government-issued ID verification
- Facial recognition verification
- Trading limits: Up to 100 BTC per day

### Level 3 - Enhanced Verification
- Proof of address
- Source of funds declaration
- Unlocked withdrawal limits
- Trading limits: Unlimited

## Verification Process

### Basic Verification (Level 1)

1. Register with your email address
2. Verify your email through the confirmation link
3. Add and verify your phone number
4. Complete basic profile information

### Identity Verification (Level 2)

1. Navigate to "Account" > "Verification" > "Identity Verification"
2. Select your country of residence
3. Upload required documents:
   - Valid government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID)
   - Take a selfie for facial verification
4. Wait for approval (typically 1-2 business days)

### Enhanced Verification (Level 3)

1. Complete Level 2 verification first
2. Navigate to "Account" > "Verification" > "Enhanced Verification"
3. Upload proof of address (utility bill, bank statement not older than 3 months)
4. Complete the source of funds declaration form
5. Wait for approval (typically 3-5 business days)

## Document Requirements

### Acceptable ID Documents
- Passport
- Driver's license
- National ID card
- Residence permit

Documents must be:
- Valid and not expired
- Clearly visible with all four corners showing
- Free from glare or reflections
- Unaltered and unmodified

### Acceptable Proof of Address Documents
- Utility bill (electricity, water, gas)
- Bank statement
- Tax statement
- Internet or phone bill

Documents must be:
- Less than 3 months old
- Show your full name and address
- Issued by an official organization
- In color and unaltered

## Verification FAQs

### Why do I need to verify my account?

Verification helps us:
- Comply with global regulations
- Prevent fraud
- Protect our users
- Provide higher deposit and withdrawal limits

### How long does verification take?

- Level 1: Immediate
- Level 2: Typically 1-2 business days
- Level 3: Typically 3-5 business days

During peak periods, verification may take longer.

### What if my verification is rejected?

If your verification is rejected, you will receive a notification with the reason. Common reasons include:
- Blurry or unclear documents
- Expired documents
- Information mismatch
- Document tampering suspicion

You can resubmit your verification with corrected documents.
