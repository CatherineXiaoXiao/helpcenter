# Referring Program

Earn rewards by inviting friends to join CoinByte.

## How It Works

1. Share your referral code
2. Friends sign up using your code
3. They complete verification
4. Both receive rewards when they trade

## Your Referral Benefits

- Get 30% of your friends' trading fees
- Lifetime commission
- No limit on referrals
- Instant rewards distribution

## Friend's Benefits

- Trading fee discount
- Welcome bonus
- Special promotions
- Instant account setup

## How to Refer

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Referral Program"
3. Get your unique code
4. Share via:
   - Direct link
   - QR code
   - Social media
   - Email

## Track Your Earnings

- View total referrals
- Check commission earned
- Monitor friend's activity
- Download earnings report

## Terms & Conditions

- Valid for new users only
- KYC verification required
- Minimum trading volume applies
- Commission paid in original currency
