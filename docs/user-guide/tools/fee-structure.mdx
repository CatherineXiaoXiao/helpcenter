      <td style={{ padding: '12px' }}>
        <strong>Fee for spot trading = Fee rate × Amount of crypto bought when order was filled.</strong><br /><br />
        Take USDT/USD as an example. If trader A (0.1% maker fee and 0.15% taker fee, spread is 0.02%) buys 1 USDT at the market price of 100 USD, A’s trading fee = (0.15% + 0.02%) × 1 = 0.0017 USDT.<br />
        A receives (1 - 0.0017) = 0.9983 USDT.<br /><br />
        If A sells 1 USDT at a limit price and buys 100 USD, A is considered the maker.<br />
        A’s fee = (0.1% + 0.02%) × 100 = 0.06 USD. A receives 99.94 USD after deduction.<br /><br />
        <strong>Please note that we provide one of the lowest spreads, but the spread will vary based on the specific Crypto Asset and market liquidity.</strong>
      </td>
