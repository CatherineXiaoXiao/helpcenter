# App Notification

Set up market alerts to receive app notifications for significant market changes and price fluctuations of up to 10 of our most popular assets. This feature is exclusively available on our mobile app.

## How to turn on market alerts for the CoinByte app

### **App**

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select the <strong>green CoinByte icon</strong> in the top left to open the Personal Profile page.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Notification</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>
