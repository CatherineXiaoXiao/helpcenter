# Add or remove a crypto withdrawal wallet address

All new crypto withdrawal addresses must be confirmed by email or Google Authenticator.

## How to add a wallet address

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="Mobile App" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Withdraw</strong>.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Crypto currency withdrawal</strong>, then select the cryptocurrency you want to withdraw.</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1558151490/d1b089af36d9a4c3579f3b582c13/image.png?expires=1749159900&signature=9acfc534c528466a3d2a44314c9bf4e5b64517b3379cca4d356d00f9791360f0&req=dSUiHsh7nIVWWfMW1HO4zQA%2BQZL2ivTWdC1fwZLRDX1T%2FMMXttw3vqz8HO5j%0AoUjw%0A" alt="Select crypto to withdraw" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

<li>Select <strong>Please select an address</strong>.</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1558153407/f82d1004605506cd6ec987f040e6/image.png?expires=1749159900&signature=8aaf7ca675eea640f0edab5c4e8974a753d4f2e734276d21eeb5993eb6d3c34f&req=dSUiHsh7noVfXvMW1HO4zcmLmA1OgiEhQOU3%2B8czkHTsi7KTMrjcoMpuutGP%0Auewm%0A" alt="Add withdrawal address button" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

<li>Paste your withdrawal address or scan the code.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Confirm</strong>; you need to do 2FA verification to finish.</li>
</RoundStepList>

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="Web Platform" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Asset</strong> on the header.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Withdraw</strong> on the left-hand side menu.</li>
<li>Select the cryptocurrency you want to withdraw.</li>
<li>Select <strong>add an address</strong> to turn to Address Management page.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Add a withdrawal address</strong>.</li>
<li>Paste your withdrawal address from other platforms.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Confirm</strong>, then enter the email and Google Authenticator codes.</li>
</RoundStepList>

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## How to remove a wallet address

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="Mobile App" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **App**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Withdraw</strong>.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Crypto currency withdrawal</strong>, then select the cryptocurrency you want to withdraw.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Please select an address</strong> to go to Address Management page.</li>
<li><strong>Swipe left</strong> the address you want to remove, then select <strong>"delete"</strong>.</li>
</RoundStepList>

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="Web Platform" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### **Web**

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Asset</strong> on the header.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Withdraw</strong> on the left-hand side menu.</li>
<li>Select the cryptocurrency you want to withdraw.</li>
<li>Select <strong>add an address</strong> to go to Address Management page.</li>
<li>Select <strong>delete</strong> for the address you want to remove.</li>
</RoundStepList>

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
