Withdrawing Australian dollars from CoinByte to your bank account is a simple process.

If this is your first withdrawal, you will need to:

<TickList>
  <li><strong><a href="/helpcenter/docs/deposits-withdrawals/bank-account/link-a-bank-account">link a bank account</a></strong> to your CoinByte account before withdrawing funds.</li>
  <li><strong>For personal accounts:</strong> Withdrawals must be made to your personal bank account.</li>
  <li><strong>For corporate accounts:</strong> Withdrawals must be made to the bank account linked to that company.</li>
</TickList>

<WarningBox>
💡 <strong>Please note:</strong><br/>
You’ll need to complete a deposit of at least 10 AUD first to verify the authenticity of your withdrawal account. You can learn <strong><a href="/helpcenter/docs/deposits-withdrawals/deposit-fiat-asset/deposit-australian-dollars">Deposit Australian dollars</a></strong> to learn how to deposit AUD.
</WarningBox>

## How to withdraw Australian dollars

<Tabs groupId="platform">
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile App">

<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

### App

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
  <li>Select <strong>Withdraw</strong> at the <strong>Dashboard</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Fiat</strong>, then select <strong>AUD</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the amount you want to withdraw, select your bank account. If you haven’t linked a bank account, you can select <strong>add bank account</strong> to link a bank account.</li>
  <li>Select continue to finish.</li>
  <li><strong>Please note:</strong> If this is your first withdrawal on CoinByte and you haven’t made any deposits before, you’ll need to complete a deposit of at least 10 AUD first to verify the authenticity of your withdrawal account.</li>
</RoundStepList>

## How long do AUD withdrawals take?

- Within a minute if your bank uses Osko.
- 2 business days if your bank doesn't use Osko.

<WarningBox>
💡 <strong>Please note:</strong><br/>
<TickList>
  <li>An AUD withdrawal cannot be canceled.</li>
  <li>If you enter incorrect bank details when setting up a fiat withdrawal address, the withdrawal is likely to be rejected. We will need to wait for 2–3 business days for the withdrawal to be returned before taking any further action.</li>
</TickList>
</WarningBox>

## Limits

<InfoBox>
Limits include both cash and crypto transactions.
</InfoBox>

- **Daily Withdrawal Limit:** $50k AUD  
- **Minimum Withdrawal:** $50 AUD each withdrawal.
