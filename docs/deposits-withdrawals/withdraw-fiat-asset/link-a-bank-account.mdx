# Link a bank account

If this is your first time making a withdrawal you’ll need to add a bank account before you can withdraw funds.

<WarningBox>
💡
<TickList>
<li><strong>Please Note:</strong> to protect your asset, you have to complete your first deposit (Minimum 10 AUD) to verify your bank account.</li>
</TickList>
</WarningBox>

You can also remove an outdated bank account to avoid accidentally withdrawing to the wrong account.

<TickList>
<li>For personal accounts, withdrawals must be made to your personal bank account.</li>
<li>For entity accounts, such as those belonging to a trust, company, or SMSF, withdrawals must go to the bank account linked to that entity.</li>
</TickList>

<WarningBox>
💡
<TickList>
<li>Please ensure that you enter your bank details accurately. If you provide incorrect bank details and proceed with a withdrawal, recovering the funds (if possible) may take a considerable amount of time.</li>
<li>For personal accounts, withdrawals must be made to your personal bank account.</li>
<li>For entity accounts, such as those belonging to a trust, company, or SMSF, withdrawals must go to the bank account linked to that entity.</li>
</TickList>
</WarningBox>

## How to link a bank account

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="app" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Withdraw</strong> at the <strong>Dashboard</strong>.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Fiat deposit</strong>, then select <strong>AUD</strong> or <strong>NZD</strong>.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Add Bank Account</strong>, the page will jump to <strong>Deposit</strong>.</li>

<figure style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/t32wgff3/1557937369/817dda2d24efb4c624ba3b19e0e6/image.png?expires=1749150000&signature=5f3e73703d0367c89179628b5bca88c1becefe530c41128c73e37dee8f41a074&req=dSUiEcB9moJZUPMW1HO4zayeSCqctuaWGW7i80w3qxTDjouhiuHF83gNI%2FI2%0AVsmc%0A" alt="Add bank account on app" style={{ width: 300 }} />
</figure>

<li>Enter a deposit amount (minimum 10 AUD) to verify your account.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Continue</strong>, copy your unique deposit information.</li>
<li><strong>Verification:</strong> Log into your bank and use the deposit information to finish the verification deposit.</li>
</RoundStepList>

<div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}></div>
</TabItem>

<TabItem value="web" label="Web">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

<RoundStepList>
<li>Select <strong>Asset</strong> on the header.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Withdraw</strong> on the left hand side menu.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Verify Now</strong>, the page will jump to <strong>Deposit</strong>.</li>
