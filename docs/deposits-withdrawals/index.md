# Deposits & Withdrawals

Learn how to safely and quickly deposit and withdraw cryptocurrencies and fiat currencies on the CoinByte platform.

## Supported Assets

CoinByte supports deposits and withdrawals for various assets:

- **Cryptocurrencies**: BTC, ETH, USDT, SOL and other major cryptocurrencies
- **Fiat Currencies**: CNY, USD, EUR, GBP, JPY and more

Each asset has specific deposit and withdrawal channels, processing times, and fee structures.

## Processing Times

Different types of deposits and withdrawals have different processing times:

| Asset Type | Deposit Processing Time | Withdrawal Processing Time |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Cryptocurrency | Depends on network confirmations, typically minutes to hours | Usually within 1 hour, maximum 24 hours |
| Fiat Currency | Bank Transfer: 1-3 business days<br/>Electronic Payments: Instant to hours | Bank Transfer: 1-5 business days<br/>Electronic Payments: Instant to 24 hours |

## Fee Structure

CoinByte charges different fees for various deposit and withdrawal methods:

- **Cryptocurrency Deposits**: Most are free, some networks may charge small network fees
- **Cryptocurrency Withdrawals**: Fixed or percentage fees based on network and asset type
- **Fiat Deposits**: Some methods are free, others charge 0.5-2% processing fees
- **Fiat Withdrawals**: 0.5-2% processing fees depending on withdrawal method and amount

## Security Notes

To ensure the safety of your funds when making deposits and withdrawals:

- Carefully verify deposit addresses, incorrect addresses may result in loss of funds
- Use networks and deposit methods supported by the platform
- Complete identity verification and security verification for first-time withdrawals
- Large withdrawals may require additional security reviews

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **What should I do if my deposit hasn't arrived?**<br/>Check the transaction confirmation status and contact customer support with your transaction hash (TXID).
  
- **Why was my withdrawal rejected?**<br/>Possible reasons include failed security verification, exceeding limits, or account abnormalities.
  
- **How can I track the status of my deposits and withdrawals?**<br/>View the status and details of all transactions in "Wallet" -> "Transaction History".
