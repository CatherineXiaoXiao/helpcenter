# Deposit Australian Dollars

Learn how to deposit AUD into your CoinByte account.

## Available Deposit Methods

### PayID/Osko
- Instant deposits
- No fees
- Available 24/7
- Minimum deposit: 10 AUD

### Direct Bank Transfer
- Processing time: 1-3 business days
- No fees
- Minimum deposit: 50 AUD

### POLi
- Instant deposits
- Small service fee
- Available for most Australian banks
- Minimum deposit: 10 AUD

## How to Deposit

1. Log in to your CoinByte account
2. Go to "Wallet" > "Deposit"
3. Select "AUD"
4. Choose your preferred deposit method
5. Follow the instructions provided

## Important Notes

- Always use your unique reference number
- Deposits must come from an account in your name
- Keep transaction receipts
- Contact support if funds don't arrive

## Processing Times

| Method | Time |
|--------|------|
| PayID/Osko | Instant |
| Bank Transfer | 1-3 business days |
| POLi | Instant |

## Deposit Limits

- Minimum: 10 AUD
- Maximum depends on:
  - Verification level
  - Payment method
  - Account history
