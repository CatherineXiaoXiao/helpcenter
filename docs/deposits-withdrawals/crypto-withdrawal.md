# Withdraw Crypto from CoinByte

Learn how to safely withdraw your cryptocurrency from CoinByte.

## Before Withdrawing

Ensure you have:
- Completed KYC verification
- Enabled 2FA
- Added and whitelisted withdrawal addresses
- Sufficient balance (including fees)

## Withdrawal Process

1. Go to "Wallet" > "Withdraw"
2. Select the cryptocurrency
3. Choose or add withdrawal address
4. Enter withdrawal amount
5. Select network (if applicable)
6. Confirm withdrawal details
7. Complete 2FA verification

## Network Selection

Choose the correct network to avoid loss of funds:
- BTC: Bitcoin Network
- ETH/ERC20: Ethereum Network
- BNB/BEP20: Binance Smart Chain
- USDT: Multiple networks available

## Security Measures

- 24-hour lock after changing security settings
- Email confirmation required
- 2FA verification needed
- Address whitelist protection

## Withdrawal Fees

Fees vary by cryptocurrency and network:
- Bitcoin (BTC): 0.0005 BTC
- Ethereum (ETH): 0.005 ETH
- USDT (TRC20): 1 USDT
- Check current fees before withdrawal

## Processing Time

- Most withdrawals process within 30 minutes
- May take longer during high network congestion
- Status updates provided via email
