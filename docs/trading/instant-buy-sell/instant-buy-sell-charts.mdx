# Instant Buy/Sell Charts

On our platform’s buy/sell page, trading charts are available on both **App** and **Web**.

While staying on the trading page and tracking the performance of your favorite assets, you can also:

- Place orders using the order panel  
- Check your Open Orders and Order History (**Web**)
