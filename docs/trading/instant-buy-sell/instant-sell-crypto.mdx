# Instant sell crypto

Selling your crypto on CoinByte is a straightforward process. CoinByte supports most kinds of crypto selling.  
You can sell crypto instantly for <strong>Australian dollars</strong> and <strong>New Zealand dollars</strong>. Make sure that you have selected the correct base currency.

## How to instantly sell crypto on CoinByte

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="Mobile App" label="App">
<div style={{ marginTop: '2.5rem' }}></div>

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}></div>

<li>Select <strong>Sell</strong>.</li>
<li>Select the <strong>Trading pairs (Quote Currency/Base Currency)</strong> that you want to trade on the top of the page. For example, ETH/AUD.</li>
