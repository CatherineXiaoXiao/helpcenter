# Instant Buy Crypto

Learn how to instantly purchase cryptocurrency on CoinByte.

## Before You Start

Ensure you have:
- Completed KYC verification
- Added a payment method
- Sufficient funds in your account

## How to Buy Crypto

1. Open the CoinByte app
2. Tap "Buy" on the home screen
3. Select the cryptocurrency you want to buy
4. Enter the amount
5. Review the transaction details
6. Confirm your purchase

## Payment Methods

- Bank Transfer
- Credit/Debit Card
- PayID/Osko (AUD)
- POLi (NZD)

## Transaction Fees

- Standard trading fee: 0.1%
- Payment method fees vary:
  - Bank transfer: Free
  - Card payments: 2.5%
  - Other methods: Check current rates

## Price Protection

Our price protection ensures you get:
- The displayed price for 30 seconds
- Best available market rate
- Price guarantee during processing

## Transaction Limits

- Minimum purchase: 10 AUD/NZD
- Maximum limits depend on:
  - Verification level
  - Payment method
  - Account history
